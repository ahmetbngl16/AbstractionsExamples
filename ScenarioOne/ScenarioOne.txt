
You have two entities, one of them student, one of them teacher.
These entities have a name,a surname, and a national identity.
Students have a grade.
Teachers have a salary.
When a teacher logs into the system, they only log into the database.
When a student logs into the system, he logs the database and the SMS.
Solve this scenario using inheritance and polymorphism.
